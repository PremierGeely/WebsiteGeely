Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I change my oil?
Most vehicles require an oil change every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, but check your owner's manual for specifics.
What are signs my brakes need repair?
Common signs include squealing noises, longer stopping distances, or a soft brake pedal.
